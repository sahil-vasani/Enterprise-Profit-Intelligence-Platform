  KEY FINDINGS
  1. Best segment: 'Potential_Loyal' | Worst: 'Champion'
     Profit gap between them: ₹1.14M.
  2. Repeat customers drive 83.9% of total revenue.
  3. 15.2% of customers are 'At_Risk' — immediate retention needed.
  4. Best acquisition channel: 'Organic_Search' | Worst: 'Promotional_Campaign'.
  5. Champion CLV is 74371% higher than At_Risk CLV
     (₹574K vs ₹1K).

  BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
  R1. RETAIN 'At_Risk' customers (15.2% of base).
      Win-back campaigns with personalised discounts and loyalty offers.
  R2. SCALE 'Organic_Search' — highest ROI acquisition. Increase budget 20-30%.
  R3. PROTECT Champions with early access, rewards, and dedicated support.
      Their CLV is 74371% higher — churn cost is enormous.
  R4. INCREASE repeat purchase rate via post-purchase email flows
      and subscription/loyalty tier upgrades.
  R5. SHIFT spend from 'Promotional_Campaign' — lowest profit-per-order channel.
